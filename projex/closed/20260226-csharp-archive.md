## Notes

- **Plan → Log → Walkthrough triplets** are the dominant pattern, providing strong audit trails for every major implementation effort.
- **Test suite grew from 8 to ~590 tests** across this period — from initial MacroPreprocessor validation to the full compliance-audit patch wave.
- **Runtime architecture evolved in three phases:** (1) feature-by-feature verb driver additions (channels, signals, navigation), (2) HandlerRegistry refactor establishing the formal plugin architecture, (3) standard verb library built on that foundation (presentation, media).
- **Spec compliance audit drove the Feb 25 wave** — set-resolve, list-concat, math verbs, and first-verb fixes were all audit-gap closures rather than new features.
- The `20260214-storage-completion-log.md` is notably truncated; the log covers only Step 1 while the walkthrough covers the full implementation.
- The `20260215-runtime-core-formalization-plan-review.md` is the only Review-type document in this archive — an unusual pre-execution validation step that proved valuable.
